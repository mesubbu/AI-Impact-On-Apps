Play Store App AI Impact Visualizer

This research tool visualizes 318 Play Store apps across 22 categories, each scored for AI impact on a 1–10 scale. Each rectangle's area is proportional to equal weight within its category group. Color shows the selected metric — toggle between AI impact score, disruption type, and confidence level. Click any tile to search for it on the Play Store.

AI Impact Score measures how significantly AI is expected to reshape or disrupt each app's core value proposition — considering automation potential, enhancement opportunities, and competitive pressure from AI-native alternatives.
